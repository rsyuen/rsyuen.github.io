Welcome to noms! Located in San Francisco China Town, we are a team of passionate foodies dedicated to sharing our love for authentic and delicious dumplings with our Bay Area community.

Our story began with a simple idea: to create a place where people could gather and enjoy a comforting and satisfying meal together. We believe that food has the power to bring people together, and we are proud to serve our community with a wide variety of dumplings and more that are made with love and care.

Our menu features a range of traditional and modern dumpling recipes, each made with the freshest ingredients sourced from local Bay Area farmers and producers. We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing, and we believe that great food starts with great ingredients.

At our restaurant, we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes our customers feel at home. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch or celebrating a special occasion with family and friends, we're here to make your experience unforgettable.

Thank you for choosing noms in San Francisco. We look forward to serving you!
