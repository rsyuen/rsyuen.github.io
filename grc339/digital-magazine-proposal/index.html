Introduction

Archive

Archive Magazine is a publication centered around Women in Comedy and aims to document inspiring ideas, people, and movements in the comedy industry. Archive covers the trailblazers and their stories in film, television, and other forms of entertainment.The main goal of this magazine is to document past, current, and future happenings in the industry and inspire its readers, especially those who may feel under-represented in entertainment.
